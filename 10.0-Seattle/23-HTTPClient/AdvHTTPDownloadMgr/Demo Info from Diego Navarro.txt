Info from Diego:

 
If you know the download address, just use the Direct download edit, and press download.
If you want to explore the links paste an address in the first edit and press getLinks, the listbox should be filled with the available links of the page
  Button explore will try to navigate and get links of the selected address on the listbox.
  If you want to download a file from the links on the listbox just select it and press the download button
 
The downloads list is not persistent, will be lost after close, but downloaded files will be available on the documents path in each platform.
Has been tested in win, osx, android and ios

You can replace the URL with your own download URL.
